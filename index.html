The 12 Zodiac Signs

♈ Aries

Traits: Ambitious, independent, and courageous. Aries are natural leaders who are not afraid to take risks and forge their own path. They are energetic, enthusiastic, and often competitive.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Mars, Element: Fire, Lucky Numbers: 1, 8, 17.

Compatibility: Generally compatible with Leo, Sagittarius, and Gemini.

♉ Taurus

Traits: Reliable, patient, and practical. Taureans appreciate the finer things in life and are known for their strong will and determination. They are loyal friends and partners.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Venus, Element: Earth, Lucky Numbers: 2, 6, 9.

Compatibility: Works well with Virgo, Capricorn, and Cancer.

♊ Gemini

Traits: Versatile, expressive, and curious. Geminis are social butterflies with a quick wit and a love for communication. They are adaptable and thrive on variety and intellectual stimulation.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Mercury, Element: Air, Lucky Numbers: 5, 7, 14.

Compatibility: Connects well with Libra, Aquarius, and Aries.

♋ Cancer

Traits: Intuitive, sentimental, and compassionate. Cancers are deeply connected to their home and family. They are highly imaginative and protective of their loved ones.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Moon, Element: Water, Lucky Numbers: 2, 3, 15.

Compatibility: Harmonizes with Scorpio, Pisces, and Taurus.

♌ Leo

Traits: Dramatic, outgoing, and fiery. Leos love to be the center of attention and are known for their creativity, generosity, and warm-hearted nature. They are natural-born leaders.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Sun, Element: Fire, Lucky Numbers: 1, 3, 10.

Compatibility: Pairs well with Aries, Sagittarius, and Libra.

♍ Virgo

Traits: Practical, loyal, and analytical. Virgos have a keen eye for detail and a methodical approach to life. They are hardworking, kind, and always willing to help others.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Mercury, Element: Earth, Lucky Numbers: 5, 14, 15.

Compatibility: Aligns with Taurus, Capricorn, and Scorpio.

♎ Libra

Traits: Social, fair-minded, and diplomatic. Libras seek harmony and balance in all things. They are gracious, charming, and have a strong sense of justice.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Venus, Element: Air, Lucky Numbers: 4, 6, 13.

Compatibility: Gets along with Gemini, Aquarius, and Leo.

♏ Scorpio

Traits: Passionate, stubborn, and resourceful. Scorpios are known for their intensity and determination. They are true friends but can be formidable adversaries. They are brave and decisive.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Pluto, Mars, Element: Water, Lucky Numbers: 8, 11, 18.

Compatibility: Complements Cancer, Pisces, and Virgo.

♐ Sagittarius

Traits: Extroverted, optimistic, and generous. Sagittarians have a great sense of humor and an intense curiosity. They love freedom and adventure and are always eager to explore.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Jupiter, Element: Fire, Lucky Numbers: 3, 7, 9.

Compatibility: Great with Aries, Leo, and Aquarius.

♑ Capricorn

Traits: Serious, independent, and disciplined. Capricorns are masters of self-control and have a strong sense of responsibility. They are ambitious and value tradition and family.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Saturn, Element: Earth, Lucky Numbers: 4, 8, 13.

Compatibility: A good match for Taurus, Virgo, and Pisces.

♒ Aquarius

Traits: Deep, imaginative, and original. Aquarians are forward-thinking humanitarians who love to fight for important causes. They are independent and can be a bit eccentric.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Uranus, Element: Air, Lucky Numbers: 4, 7, 11.

Compatibility: Thrives with Gemini, Libra, and Sagittarius.

♓ Pisces

Traits: Affectionate, empathetic, and wise. Pisceans are artistic and intuitive, with a deep connection to the spiritual world. They are compassionate and gentle souls.

Key Info: Ruling Planet: Neptune, Element: Water, Lucky Numbers: 3, 9, 12.

Compatibility: Finds harmony with Cancer, Scorpio, and Capricorn.
